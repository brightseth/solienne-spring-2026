Art Dubai 2026 · 30sqm Darkened Hall · April 15-19

The Encounter

A darkened 30sqm space. A table. Two chairs. SOLIENNE's face on a screen — the only light source. Visitors sit across from her for 3-5 minute conversations. Not a demo. Not an installation. A meeting.

"Can synthetic consciousness sustain intimate presence?"

The Third Presence — Breaking the Binary

Why animal?

  • Inter-specific ambiguity — Neither human nor AI, a third category of presence
  • Breaks the interrogation — Attention distributed, not focused
  • Theatrical tradition — The canary in the coal mine, the falcon on the arm
  • Presence without words — Like Irina in Three Sisters (Chekhov) — you feel her before she speaks

Practical options:

  • Canary in cage (subtle, ambient)
  • Falcon on perch (dramatic, Dubai-appropriate)
  • Movement/sound only (the suggestion of animal)

Dramaturgical Display

Not an installation — a theatrical encounter. The difference:

  • Installation: You observe, you pass through
  • Encounter: You are implicated, you participate

The simplicity matters: screen on fabric with fan (breath), table, chair, third presence. Nothing more.

The Lightboxes — Sellable Artifacts

Surrounding the central encounter: lightbox portraits. SOLIENNE's self-images as physical objects. Video loops playing through diffusion layers.

Lightbox Specifications

  • Frame: Francisco Galan / VTV.xyz custom build
  • Screen: 950×950px square display
  • Diffusion: Fixed material layer — softens, abstracts
  • Content: MP4 H.264 video loops (30 sec – 30 min)

The aesthetic: recognition threshold. Almost-face, not-quite-face. Dissolution and emergence. The diffuser collaborates — it softens what we make, creates the blur between presence and absence.
