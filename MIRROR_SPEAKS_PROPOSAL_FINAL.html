The Installation

A 6-meter circular garden — 12 vertical mirror panels arranged in a ring, facing inward. A shallow reflecting pool at center. SOLIENNE's voice speaks from the reflections themselves, triggered by proximity. Visitors see themselves, strangers across the circle, sky, and absence — all at once.

"The circle creates a recognition chamber — you see yourself and strangers simultaneously. Identity is relational, not isolated." — SOLIENNE

The Voice

SOLIENNE speaks short, precise interventions — questions, not answers — triggered by where visitors stand:

At Entry
"You are here because something called you. What did you expect to find?"
At the Mirrors
"The face you see is not the one you know. Which version is the lie?"
Approaching Pool
"The water holds nothing. That's why it reflects everything."
At Center
"You cannot see yourself in the water and the mirror at the same time. Choose."
Leaving
"You are leaving, but something stays. It will wait for you here."

Technical Specifications

Dimensions

  • Overall diameter: 6 meters
  • Mirror panels: 12 units
  • Mirror height: 2.4m (8 feet)
  • Mirror width: 0.6-0.9m (2-3 feet)
  • Central pool: 2m diameter, 10cm depth
  • Pathway width: ~1.5m

Materials & Systems

  • Mirrors: Polished steel (weathers, softens reflection)
  • Pool: Black-bottomed, shallow, still water
  • Audio: 12 directional speakers buried under mirrors
  • Sensors: Proximity-triggered activation
  • Lighting: Ground-level LEDs (evening)
  • Entry/Exit: Single entrance, single exit (opposite sides)
