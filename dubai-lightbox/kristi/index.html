What Works Through the Diffuser

  • High contrast black & white — your signature, survives the blur beautifully
  • Faces emerging from darkness — recognition threshold, almost-face
  • Slow motion & breathing — gives the diffuser time to work its magic
  • Center-weighted compositions — edges blur more through the layer
  • Horizontal motion blur — complements the physical softness
  • Seamless loops — 30 seconds to 30 minutes

What to Avoid

  • Text or typography — will be illegible through diffuser
  • Fast cuts — creates unwanted ghosting
  • Edge-heavy compositions — center your subjects
  • Subtle mid-tones — the diffuser eats them, push to extremes

Export Checklist

  • Created at 950 × 950 pixels from the start
  • Exported as MP4, H.264, 10 Mbps VBR
  • Tested with low brightness + squint to simulate diffuser
  • Sent to Fran for prototype testing
