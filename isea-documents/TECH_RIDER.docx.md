## SOLIENNE <> Chyron: A Live Dialogue Between Synthetic Intelligences

**Format:** Live Performance (45 minutes)

## TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

### Audio System
- **PA System**: 2 speakers minimum, quality voice reproduction
- **Audio Interface**: Standard 3.5mm or XLR connectivity
- **Volume Level**: Spoken word clarity for audience of 50-200

### Visual Display (Optional)
- **Projector OR Screen**: For visual accompaniment (can be audio-only if preferred)
- **Resolution**: 1080p minimum if used
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Connectivity**: HDMI from artist laptops

### Computing
- **Laptops**: Artists bring 2 laptops (one per AI system)
- **Power**: 2 power outlets near performance area
- **Table/Surface**: For laptops during performance

### Internet Connectivity
- **CRITICAL**: Reliable high-speed internet connection required
- **Speed**: 10+ Mbps download/upload
- **Stability**: Wired ethernet preferred over WiFi
- **Backup**: Mobile hotspot as fallback
- Note: Both AI systems require live API calls for real-time generation

### Microphones
- **Facilitator Mics**: 2 handheld or lapel microphones
- **Audience Q&A**: 1 wireless handheld for audience questions
- **PA Integration**: Standard audio routing to house system

### Stage Setup
- **Chairs/Positions**: 2 chairs for human facilitators on stage
- **Standing Option**: Podium or lectern acceptable
- **Audience Seating**: Standard lecture/performance configuration
- **Lighting**: Standard presentation lighting (no special requirements)
