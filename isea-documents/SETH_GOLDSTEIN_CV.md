## SELECTED PROJECTS & VENTURES

**Dream Computing** — Co-founder (2024)
AI-native creative studio. Debuted at Art Basel Miami, December 2024.

**Bright Moments** — Founder (2021–2024)
Crypto art gallery operating as a DAO, pioneering "live minting" experiences that transformed generative art from isolated digital files into communal cultural events. Expanded globally to Venice, London, Berlin, Tokyo, Mexico City, and São Paulo. Established new model for artist-collector relationships in Web3.

**Turntable.fm** — Co-creator (2011)
Social music platform that pioneered avatar-based shared listening experiences, demonstrating how digital spaces could foster genuine community around culture.

**Majestic Research** — Founder (2002–2010)
Pioneered alternative data analytics for financial research, establishing new methodologies for extracting insight from unconventional data sources.

**SiteSpecific** — Founder (1995–1997)
One of the first interactive marketing agencies, at the dawn of commercial internet.
