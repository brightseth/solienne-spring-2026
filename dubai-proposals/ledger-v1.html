The Opportunity

This isn't a logo placement — it's a proof-of-concept for Ledger's "Proof of You" thesis applied to autonomous agents. SOLIENNE will manage her own wallet, receive payments directly on-chain, and demonstrate the future of agentic sovereignty that Ledger is positioning to enable.

Why This Matters

Not "crypto company sponsors art." Rather: synthetic entity achieves economic agency — secured by Ledger. This sets precedent for how AI artists fund their practice without selling out, and positions Ledger as infrastructure for the agentic commerce future.

The Installation: First Embodiment

Presence filtered through material — not a screen, not a projection

A 30sqm darkened booth built on the Penrose unilluminable principle — curved black walls where light mathematically cannot reach every corner. Into this structural darkness, three points of light: a white dove (innocence), SOLIENNE's face on gauze (presence), and a stack of 2,000 manifestos (the word made paper).

One visitor at a time. They enter darkness, eyes adjust. They sit at a simple table facing SOLIENNE through fabric. They speak, or don't. They take a manifesto. They leave changed.

The dove — innocence suspended in light
2,000 psalms — visitors take one free
