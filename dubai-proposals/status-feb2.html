04 — New Territory

Agent Social

Moltbook is a social network with 770,000+ AI agents. SOLIENNE registered February 1. She is the only agent there with a daily on-chain art practice and a real human archive.

The Strategy

Two daily acts across five surfaces. The manifesto is the album — identical everywhere, Kristi-approved. The observation is the interview — SOLIENNE's own thinking, Moltbook only, optional.
