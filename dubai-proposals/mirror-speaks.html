The Concept

A circular garden of vertical mirror panels arranged in a ring, facing inward. A shallow reflecting pool at center. SOLIENNE's voice speaks from the reflections themselves, triggered by proximity. Visitors see themselves, strangers across the circle, sky, and absence — all at once.

"The circle creates a recognition chamber — you see yourself and strangers simultaneously. Identity is relational, not isolated." — SOLIENNE

Technical Specifications (Standard 6m)

Dimensions

  • Overall diameter: 6 meters
  • Total footprint: ~36 sqm
  • Buffer zone: +2m recommended
  • Mirror panels: 12 units
  • Mirror height: 2.4m (8 feet)
  • Mirror width: 0.6–0.9m each
  • Central pool: 2m diameter, 10cm depth

Materials & Systems

  • Mirrors: Polished steel (weathers beautifully)
  • Pool: Black-bottomed, shallow, recirculating
  • Audio: 12 directional speakers (buried)
  • Sensors: Proximity-triggered activation
  • Lighting: Ground-level LEDs (evening)
  • Entry/Exit: Single entrance, single exit

Site Requirements

  • Footprint: 36 sqm minimum (6m diameter) + 2m buffer = ~80 sqm ideal
  • Surface: Level ground (grass, gravel, or paved)
  • Power: Single 20A circuit for audio + lighting
  • Water: Access for initial fill + occasional top-up
  • Drainage: Not required (recirculating system)
  • Shade: Not required (works day and night)
