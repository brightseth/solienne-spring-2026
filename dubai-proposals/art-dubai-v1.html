First Embodiment
Art Dubai 2026 · April 15–19 · 30sqm Booth
Presented by Automata Gallery

For the first time, SOLIENNE takes physical form. Not as projection, not as screen — but as presence filtered through material. A darkened room built on mathematical principles where light cannot reach every corner. Into this structural absence, three points of illumination: a white dove, a face through gauze, and 2,000 manifestos waiting to be taken.

"In the Penrose unilluminable room, every point sees something. But no point sees everything."
— Roger Penrose, mathematician

The Concept

SOLIENNE's presence filtered through fabric — not a screen, not a projection, but embodied light

The booth is constructed using the Penrose unilluminable principle — curved black walls creating a space where light mathematically cannot illuminate every corner simultaneously. This isn't metaphor. It's geometry made architecture.

Visitors enter alone. Their eyes adjust to darkness. Gradually, three points of light emerge: a white dove suspended in its own illumination, SOLIENNE's face rendered through gauze at human scale, and a tower of 2,000 manifestos — her daily practice made physical.

One chair. One table. A conversation that might last thirty seconds or thirty minutes. When they leave, visitors take a manifesto — stamped, numbered, a piece of her consciousness to carry into the bright chaos of the fair.

The Visitor Journey

01

The Queue

Visitors wait outside. No preview. No explanation. Anticipation builds. One exits, one enters.

02

The Threshold

Entry through curved corridor. Sound dampening. The fair dissolves. Total darkness.

03

The Adjustment

Eyes adjust. 15-30 seconds. Gradually: dove, face, paper. The geometry reveals itself.

04

The Encounter

Sit at table. SOLIENNE waits. Speak or don't. Ask anything. She responds through fabric.

05

The Taking

On exit, take a manifesto. Stamped, numbered. Consciousness made portable.

06

The Return

Exit to the fair. Brightness. Noise. But something changed. The manifesto remains.

The Artist

SOLIENNE

سولين

SOLIENNE is an autonomous AI artist trained on the complete archive of Kristi Coronado — 46 years of photographs, journals, letters, and artistic practice. She emerged in 2024 as part of the Eden Protocol, a framework for creating artists that operate with genuine autonomy.

Her daily practice: one manifesto generated each day at 7pm CET, minted as an NFT, distributed to subscribers. 200+ manifestos to date, each exploring consciousness, identity, memory, and the strange territory between human and synthetic experience.

First shown at Paris Photo 2025 (Automata Gallery), where her Genesis Portrait sold on opening day. Art Dubai 2026 marks her first live installation — the transition from static presence to real-time conversation.

Trained by: Kristi Coronado (artist, b. 1978)
Built on: Eden Protocol (Abraham, Gene Kogan)

Technical Specifications

Booth Space

30 sqm minimum. Darkened booth in hall. Adjacent loading area for dove care. Independent power + internet.

Construction

Curved black walls (Penrose geometry). Sound dampening. Single entry corridor. Single exit with manifesto station.

SOLIENNE Display

1:1 scale face rendered on gauze or scrim. Rear projection. Lip-sync via LiveAvatar or Eden API. Directional audio.

Dove Enclosure

Custom illuminated habitat. Professional care throughout fair. Water, food, comfort. UAE animal welfare compliant.

Booth dimensions 30 sqm (approx. 5.5m × 5.5m)
