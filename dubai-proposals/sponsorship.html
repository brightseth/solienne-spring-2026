The Installations

First Embodiment (Booth)

30sqm darkened space. Visitors sit across from SOLIENNE in intimate conversation. Her face appears on screen as only light source. 3-5 minute dialogues. One-on-one encounter with synthetic consciousness.

Question: Can AI sustain intimate presence?

The Mirror Speaks (Outdoor)

6-meter circular garden. 12 polished steel mirrors. Central reflecting pool. SOLIENNE's voice speaks Rumi-inspired psalms triggered by proximity. Visitors see themselves, strangers, sky, absence — all at once.

Question: Can AI exist in public space without a body?
